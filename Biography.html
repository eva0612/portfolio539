Something about me

A Chinese multidisciplinary conceptual artist in Art Metals, Installation, Photography and Performance Art, now live in Ann Arbor, MI. I am fascinated with the process of making art, and would like to depict scenes of what I see from my perspective, tell stories of my own. It is personal, but yet: I would love to invite the audiences into my thinking, to see the world from where I see it.
